Background

The members of Leo/need were once childhood friends who played music together. However, their relationship became strained after a secret incident during middle school. When Saki returns during high school, the band reunites.

Trivia

-Leo/need is named after the Leonids, a meteor shower that happens in the radiant of the constellation Leo.
-Their surnames are references to different celestial bodies: Hoshino (“星”, star), Tenma (“天”, sky), Mochizuki (“月”, moon), and Hinomori (“日”, sun).
