Background

Hanasato Minori has dreamed of becoming an idol since seeing Kiritani Haruka on TV. After Haruka retires from being an idol, the two manage to become friends. Soon, Minori convinces her to return to idolhood, forming MORE MORE JUMP! along with Momoi Airi and Hinomori Shizuku.

Trivia

-The group's members have surnames that are references to plants: Hanasato (“花”, flower), Kiritani (“桐”, empress tree), Momoi (“桃”, peach), and Hinomori (“森”, forest).
