Background

The circle was created by K (Kanade), in hopes of making music that "saves people" and makes them happy. She recruits the other members, Enanan (Ena), Amia (Mizuki), and Yuki (Mafuyu), communicating through a chat tool called "Nightcord." All members were unaware of each other's real names or appearances

Trivia

-The only 25-ji, Nightcord de. member who regularly attends school during the day is Mafuyu. While the other members are enrolled in school, Kanade takes her classes online, Ena takes night classes, and Mizuki often skips class.
-25-ji, Nightcord de. is the only unit where their members use stage names.
-The members have surnames that reference different times of day: Yoisaki (“宵”, night), Akiyama (“暁”, the moment right before sunrise), Shinonome (“東雲”, dawn), and Asahina (“朝”, morning).
